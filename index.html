Computer Hardware

Motherboard

The motherboard is one of the most important parts in a computer, the motherboard has a slot for the CPU which is another vital part of the computer, without the motherboard you would not be able to use your computer.

CPU

Speaking of the CPU (Central Processing Unit), the job of the CPU is to tell the computer what to do, the CPU also provides the needed processing power to complete the task. Without the CPU your computer will not function.

Ram

Next item on the list is RAM, the ram allows you to perform the common tasks. If you have a small amount of ram, your computer will run slower. Ram stands for Random Access Memory. You use RAM while doing anything on your computer.

Computer Power Supply

The next item on the list is the computer power supply. This is what allows your computer to turn on. The power supply is the small box in the computer that converts the electricity into power used for your computer.

Hard Drive

The next item on the list is the Hard Drive, the hard drive is what allows you to store all your things on your PC. Hard Drives are cheaper than a SSD but is more prone to being damaged when accidentally dropped.

DVD Drive

The next item on the list is DVD Drives, even though they are outdated and a waste of room in a PC. The DVD drive is the way we could watch movies or listen to music with. Some drives allow you to put your own content on the disc by burning it. Not with a lighter, but with computer.

Computer Case

The next item is the Computer Case. Computer Cases come in different shapes and sizes, some able to fit more fans, some just look completely absurd. The Case holds all your PC parts while in style. Some people build their own cases too. The customization of them is insane.

Video Card/Graphics Card

The next item on the list is the Video Card, also referred to as the Graphics Card. They are responsible for providing graphics to the monitor. One company that has been dominating the graphics industry is NVIDIA, if you ever heard of RTX 3090 or 4090 they made that.

Sound Card

The next item on the list is the sound card. The sound card is what lets you hear your audio from your computer. It provides the audio to your headphones or stereo system. They aren’t as common as they were back then.

Network Card

The network card is the next item on our list. The network card is what allows your computer to connect to the internet. Now they are usually built into the motherboard. You can’t really use the internet without connecting to the network somehow.

Keyboard

The next item on the list is the keyboard. I used the keyboard to code this webpage. The keyboard is one of the most important parts of the computer. Without it you would have the greatest time staring at your login screen.

Mouse

The next item on the list is the mouse, the mouse is another important item of the computer. It’s how you click around your screen. Like the computer case mice can come in many different shapes and sizes. Some are lighter than others and some have extra buttons.

Monitor

The monitor is our final item on the list. The monitor is how you see all your computer parts come together allowing you to enjoy your computer. All the parts in your case are working together for you, so appreciate them. Monitors also come in different ways. Some can handle higher fps and some have better resolution.
